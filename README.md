## "Where Was This?" photo game

A quick photo-guessing game (the **Play** button in the header, next to Record).

**How it picks locations.** The game only draws from locations that are both
tagged `visited` **and** have at least one photo — your own pins and your
photo/notes on the built-in surveyed sites both count. Each round shows one
random photo from a randomly chosen location and offers two names: the correct
one and a decoy. The decoy is preferred from the **same region** as the answer
(so it's plausible, not a giveaway), and the same location never appears as the
answer twice in a row. Photos are shown at random; the game just avoids
repeating a photo until it has run through the others.

**Scoring.** Your score is the current run's streak of correct answers. A wrong
answer ends the run. The **high score is your best-ever streak** (not a total),
and it persists. Get through *every* photo in your atlas without a miss and you
win. The high score is stored only on this device (`urbexAtlasGame.v1`) and is
never written into or restored from the GitHub-synced data.
